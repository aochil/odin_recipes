Takis Burrito Recipe

High Protein High Calorie Meal, great for bulking season.

This is what your final product should look like, pre-rolling of course. The takis add the extra kick to the flavor and is crunchy, thus making the eating experience more enjoyable.

Ingredients:

  • 1 canned tuna
  • 1 tortilla
  • 1/2 tomatoe
  • 1/2cucumber
  • 1/2 onion
  • mayonaise
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • 1 small takis bag

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Chop up the vegetables into small pieces.
  2. Open the can of tuna and drain out the water.
  3. Place the vegetables and tuna in a bowl to mix.
  4. Throw in one spoonful of mayonaise into the bowl.
  5. Add a pinch of black pepper and salt. Mix thoroughly.
  6. Place a tortilla on a plate, and put the mix ontop.
  7. Roll up the tortilla and enjoy!
