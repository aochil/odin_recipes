Rice Cake Snack

One of my favorite pre-workout snacks, is the peanut butter honey rice cakes. Great taste, good carbs, fast and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Any flavor rice cake
  • Peanut butter
  • Honey
  • Optional:Banana slices

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Place the rice cake on a plate.
  2. Gently spread peanut butter on top of rice cake.
  3. Add honey on top, make sure not to put too much.
  4. Enjoy!
