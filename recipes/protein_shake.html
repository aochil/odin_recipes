500 Calorie Very Berry Protein Shake

Easy to drink, great way to reach calorie intake for the day.

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz water
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 2 cups frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup plain low-fat yogurt
  • 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
  • 1 tbsp walmuts
  • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Get a nice blender and throw in all the ingredients.
  2. Blend it thoroughly, so it is easy to drink.
  3. CHUG! CHUG! CHUG!
  4. Looking way bigger already!
